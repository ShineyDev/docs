Thickness¶

class screen.controls.primitives.Thickness(left, top=None, right=None, bottom=None)¶

Represents the thickness of a frame around a rectangle.

Parameters

  • left (int) – The left thickness.

  • top (int) – The top thickness. Defaults to left.

  • right (int) – The right thickness. Defaults to left.

  • bottom (int) – The bottom thickness. Defaults to top.
